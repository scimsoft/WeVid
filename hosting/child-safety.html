WeVid Child Safety Standards

Last updated: July 23, 2026

WeVid, operated by Scimsoft, is committed to keeping its platform safe and has zero tolerance for child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE) in any form.

Prohibited content and behavior

The following are strictly forbidden on WeVid and lead to immediate content removal and account termination:

  • Child sexual abuse material (CSAM) of any kind, real or simulated
  • Sexualization, grooming, or solicitation of minors
  • Sharing, requesting, or linking to any such material
  • Any other content or behavior that endangers children

Enforcement

  • Reported content is reviewed promptly; violating content is removed and the responsible account is permanently banned.
  • Confirmed CSAM is reported to the relevant authorities, including the National Center for Missing & Exploited Children (NCMEC) and applicable local law enforcement, in accordance with the law.
  • We comply with all applicable child safety laws in the regions where WeVid is available.

Reporting

Users can report content and accounts directly in the app. You can also reach our child safety contact at curamipc@gmail.com. Reports are handled with priority and confidentiality.
