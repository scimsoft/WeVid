Delete your WeVid account

To delete your WeVid account and all data associated with it, send an email to curamipc@gmail.com from the Google account you use to sign in to WeVid, with the subject "WeVid account deletion request".

What gets deleted

  • Your account and profile (username, display name)
  • All videos you posted to the public feed
  • All video messages you sent
  • Push notification tokens and other device data

Deletion is permanent and processed within 30 days of your request. Crash diagnostics already collected are anonymous and cannot be linked back to your account.

See also our Privacy Policy.
